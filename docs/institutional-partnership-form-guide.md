WHAT THIS FORM IS — AND WHO IT IS FOR
This form is not for individual participants.
This is a B2B partnership inquiry form for organizational leaders who want to bring MEMOIVA Circle to their community — senior centers, hospitals, churches, VA programs, memory care facilities, Latino community organizations, and employers.
Someone completing this form is opening a business conversation with RAMP Linguistic Society LLC, not enrolling in the program themselves.
Place this form on a dedicated 'Partner With Us' or 'Bring MEMOIVA Circle to Your Community' page on your website.
HOW TO BUILD THIS FORM IN GOOGLE FORMS
Step 1: Create the form
→  Go to forms.google.com and sign in
→  Click + for a new blank form
→  Title: Bring MEMOIVA Circle to Your Community — Partnership Inquiry
→  Paste the description text below into the form description field
Step 2: Add each question
→  Match question types exactly as listed below
→  Mark required questions as required using the toggle
→  Use Multiple choice for single-select, Checkboxes for multi-select
Step 3: Set up email notification so you are alerted instantly
→  Click the three-dot menu at the top right of the form editor
→  Click 'Get email notifications for new responses'
→  Every inquiry goes directly to your email — respond within 24 hours
Step 4: Get your link for the website
→  Click Send → chain link icon → Shorten URL → Copy
→  Paste this link into your website's Partner With Us page
→  Also embed the form directly on the page if your website builder allows it

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER SOMEONE SUBMITS
Your response protocol — follow this every time:
Within 2 business days:  Send a personal email acknowledging receipt and proposing a 20-minute discovery call
Discovery call:  Learn their population, timeline, budget, and decision process — do not pitch pricing on the first call
Follow-up within 48 hours:  Send a one-page Partnership Proposal tailored to their organization — pricing, structure, and next steps
Close:  Execute a simple Letter of Agreement and invoice for the block of seats
Institutional partners are your highest-value revenue channel — one hospital system or VA program can fill multiple cohorts simultaneously. Every inquiry deserves a same-day acknowledgment even if the full response takes 48 hours.
