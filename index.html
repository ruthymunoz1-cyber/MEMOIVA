The Science

This is not just a wellness class. It is a research-informed cognitive wellness program.

Every structural decision in MEMOIVA Circle was informed by peer-reviewed research for adults 50+ — grounded in the specific science of bilingualism and cognitive wellness.

4.5
years later

Bilingual adults develop dementia symptoms years later than monolinguals

Multiple peer-reviewed studies — including Venugopal et al., Alzheimer's & Dementia, 2024 — show that adults who engage in bilingual language practice develop symptoms of Alzheimer's and dementia an average of 4.5–5 years later than monolinguals. MEMOIVA Circle is among the first facilitated programs in the U.S. designed to put this research to work for adults 50+ in a structured, community setting.

2×
neural pathways

A word learned in two languages creates two neural pathways — not one

Research from Concordia University shows bilingual individuals demonstrate significantly greater hippocampal neuroplasticity than monolinguals. MEMOIVA's dual-language vocabulary approach activates both pathways every session — giving your brain the opportunity to build connections in two languages — which research associates with greater brain resilience.

MEMOIVA Circle is inspired by peer-reviewed research on bilingualism and cognitive health. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical condition. Please consult a qualified healthcare provider with any concerns about your cognitive health.

How It Works

Eight weeks. Twice a week. One circle at a time.

MEMOIVA Circle runs in small cohorts — maximum 6 students — who move through the program together. Here is what one 8-week block looks like.

01
Every Week — Twice

Two live sessions, 60 minutes each

Each week brings two live sessions with your facilitator and your circle. New language, songs, and cognitive engagement every session — structured to build on itself week after week.

02
Every Day

Practice that travels with you

Your student workbook and the MEMOIVA digital app keep your mind active between sessions — with vocabulary practice, brain health exercises, digital coloring, and weekly self-check-ins.

03
Week 8

Graduation — with real results

Every cohort ends with a graduation. You will see your own progress — measurable, documented, and yours. Then decide if you want to continue your language journey into the next circle.

04
Every Session

A closing that means something

Every MEMOIVA Circle session closes the same way — together, in both languages, with an affirmation of who you are becoming. It is the moment students say they look forward to most.

About

Why MEMOIVA exists

MEMOIVA Circle is the flagship program of RAMP Linguistic Society — a woman veteran-owned language and technology company built on a simple belief: that the brain never stops growing, and that language is one of the most powerful ways to protect it.

It was inspired by her grandmother living with dementia who wanted to learn a new language — and kept trying, with everything she had — at a time when the right tools simply didn't exist.

But now MEMOIVA Circle is bridging the gap and exists specifically for adults 50+ who are navigating cognitive decline, are concerned about their brain health, or are simply determined to keep their minds sharp. And for the families and caregivers who love them.

Grounded in peer-reviewed research on bilingualism and cognitive wellness, it's the program that should have existed for her grandmother — and for every student like her.

Rigorous. Warm. Dignity-forward. Those are the RAMP Linguistic Society and MEMOIVA standards.

In every word. Every session. Every circle.
